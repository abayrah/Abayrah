Shipping & Delivery

Our commitment to delivering your bespoke pieces with care

Order Processing

At ABAYRAH, we believe in the art of precision and elegance. As many of our silhouettes are crafted with bespoke attention to detail, please allow our atelier 2 to 4 business days to process, meticulously inspect, and hand-package your order before it is dispatched.

Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will begin processing on the following business day. Once your order has been prepared, you will receive an official confirmation email containing your tracking itinerary.

Domestic Delivery (India)

We are pleased to offer secure and swift delivery across India. We partner with premium logistics couriers to ensure your package arrives in pristine condition.

  • Standard Delivery: 4 - 6 business days after dispatch.
  • Express Delivery: Available for select pin codes (2 - 3 business days).
  • Shipping Fees: Enjoy complimentary standard shipping on all domestic orders over ₹2,500. A nominal handling fee applies to orders below this threshold.

International Delivery

ABAYRAH is proud to drape women across the globe. International shipments are securely routed via our global logistics partners.

  • Global Standard: 7 - 14 business days, depending on the destination country.
  • Customs & Duties: Please note that international orders may be subject to local customs duties and import taxes. These charges are determined by your local customs authority and are the sole responsibility of the customer. ABAYRAH cannot predict or cover these fees.

Tracking Your Itinerary

Once your elegant pieces have been handed over to our shipping concierge, you will receive an email update with a live tracking link. You may also track the status of your delivery directly through your Personal Vault by logging into your account on our storefront.

Package Integrity & Security

Every ABAYRAH parcel is sealed with our signature tampering-proof packaging. Upon receiving your delivery, we kindly request you to inspect the exterior. If the package appears compromised or tampered with, please do not accept the delivery and contact our concierge immediately.

Need Assistance?

Our concierge team is at your disposal for any delivery inquiries.
